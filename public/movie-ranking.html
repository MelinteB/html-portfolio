The Best Movies According to Bogdan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this crime drama is often regarded as one of the greatest films in cinematic history. It tells the story of the Corleone crime family and features iconic performances by Marlon Brando and Al Pacino.

Citizen Kane (1941)

Directed by and starring Orson Welles, "Citizen Kane" is known for its innovative storytelling techniques and deep exploration of the life of a media mogul. It's often considered a masterpiece of cinema.

Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont, this film is based on a Stephen King novella and follows the story of a man wrongfully imprisoned for murder. It's celebrated for its themes of hope and redemption.
